Alignment

Amazon

  • Amazon.com

Amazon follows the alignment principle. This is because the boxes shown are aligned into a grid consisting of three columns and three rows for that specific section.

Contrast

W3schools

  • W3schools.com

W3schools is a good example of contrasts in website design. Whereever there are dark colors, light colors are used and vice versa. This makes it possible for easy readabilty and navigation. It is also pleasant to look at.

Visual Hierachy

Doordash

  • Doordash

When looking at the "Doordash" website, it is easy to see what the designers want us to see. This is because of the way they have ordered the way specific information are presented that helps capture attention.
