American Fork, Utah

background

American Fork Chamber of Commerce has served the residence of Amercian Fork since the year 1980. Founded but members of the community to help support local businesses. Our support ranges from career to growth opportunity in the industry. American fork is our first priority.

demographic

As of the census[4] of 2010, there were 26,263 people in 7,098 households residing in the city. The population density was 2,853.7 people per square mile (1,101.5/km2).
